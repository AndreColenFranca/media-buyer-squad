## 14. Production Guidelines

### Do's

- ✅ Start with strong hook (first 3 seconds)
- ✅ Use captions (80% watch without sound)
- ✅ Show faces (builds trust)
- ✅ Keep it authentic (avoid overly polished)
- ✅ Test multiple hooks
- ✅ Include clear CTA

### Don'ts

- ❌ Bury the hook (get to it immediately)
- ❌ Use generic stock footage
- ❌ Make it too long (attention span is short)
- ❌ Forget mobile optimization
- ❌ Use misleading claims
- ❌ Violate Meta ad policies

### Meta Ad Policies Compliance

- [ ] No prohibited content
- [ ] No misleading claims
- [ ] Proper disclosures (if required)
- [ ] No before/after images (if health/fitness)
- [ ] Text in image < 20% (if applicable)
